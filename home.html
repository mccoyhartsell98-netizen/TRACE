BUILT FOR

From the admin panel to the shop floor kiosk, every part of TRACE was built with one thing in mind — speed and clarity when it matters most.

// Why Choose TRACE

EVERYTHING
YOU NEED.

No bloat. No learning curve. Just a system that works the second your team scans their first job.

QR Scan & Move

Scan any job label and move it to a lane or machine in seconds. Works on any device — no app install required.

Real-Time Updates

Every move, every status change — reflected instantly across every screen in your facility. No refresh needed.

Role-Based Access

Forklift drivers, operators, supervisors, and admins — each role sees exactly what they need and nothing they don't.

Kiosk Mode

A dedicated PIN-protected kiosk interface built for the shop floor. Simple enough to use with gloves on.

Activity Feed

A live log of every job movement. Know who moved what, when, and where — with full audit history.

Built-In Assistant

Ask the TRACE assistant anything — "Where is job 78011?" or "What's in Lane 3?" — and get an instant answer.
